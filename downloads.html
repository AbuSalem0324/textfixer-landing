Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update to the latest version?

To update your TextFixer shortcut, simply download the latest version and install it. Your access code will be preserved automatically.

Is TextFixer available for Android?

Currently, TextFixer is only available as an iOS Shortcut. We're exploring options for Android and will announce when it's available.

Can I use TextFixer on multiple devices?

Yes! Your subscription allows you to use TextFixer on multiple iOS devices using the same access code.

What happens if I exceed my free tier limits?

If you exceed your monthly free tier limits (20 fixes or 500 characters per fix), you'll receive an error message. You can either wait until your limits reset or upgrade to the Pro plan for unlimited usage.
