About The Story Behind TextFixer

Why I Created TextFixer

My name is Adam Szaloczi, and I created TextFixer to solve a problem that was driving me crazy: writing longer texts or comments and constantly worrying about imperfect writing.

You know that feeling when you're crafting an important email, writing a social media post, or leaving a comment, and you're second-guessing every sentence? "Does this sound professional? Did I make any typos? Is this clear enough?"

I found myself constantly switching between apps, copying text to grammar checkers, or relying on separate tools that broke my writing flow. It was frustrating and time-consuming.

I wanted to write more confidently without the mental overhead of switching apps or interrupting my thought process. The solution seemed simple: just write, and if I'm not sure about something, fix it right then and there - without leaving the app I'm already in.

So I built TextFixer as an iOS Shortcut that works everywhere. Select any text, tap share, choose TextFixer, and boom - your text is instantly improved. No app switching, no copying and pasting, no breaking your flow.

Under the hood, TextFixer relies on OpenAI's ChatGPT 3.5, which provides the perfect balance between quick response times and reliable results. This proven AI technology ensures your text corrections are both fast and intelligent, understanding context and nuance rather than just checking basic grammar rules.

It started working so well for me that I wanted to share it with others. Turns out, I wasn't the only one with this problem! Now TextFixer helps thousands of people write with confidence every day.

Whether you're a professional sending client emails, a student writing assignments, or just someone who wants their social media posts to look polished - TextFixer is here to help you communicate better, faster.

– Adam Szaloczi, Founder of TextFixer

TextFixer Now Available as Browser Extension

We're excited to announce that TextFixer is now available as a browser extension for Chrome, Edge, and all major Chromium-based browsers! This means you can now get perfect grammar and spelling corrections across all your web-based work.

The browser extension brings the same AI-powered text correction you love to every website you visit. Whether you're composing emails, filling out forms, or crafting social media posts, TextFixer is now just a right-click away.

Works Across All Your Browsers

The TextFixer extension is available through the Chrome Web Store and works perfectly with:

  • Google Chrome - The world's most popular browser
  • Microsoft Edge - Microsoft's modern, fast browser
  • Brave - Privacy-focused browsing with ad blocking
  • Opera - Feature-rich browser with built-in VPN
  • Vivaldi - Highly customizable browser for power users
  • Arc - The innovative new browser experience

Any Chromium-based browser that supports Chrome Web Store extensions will work seamlessly with TextFixer.

Perfect for Web-Based Work

The browser extension shines in scenarios where you spend most of your time online:

Web Applications: Working in Google Docs, Notion, or any web-based productivity tool? TextFixer integrates seamlessly with text fields in web apps, giving you instant corrections without breaking your flow.

Email Everywhere: Whether you're using Gmail, Outlook Web, or any other web-based email client, TextFixer ensures your professional communication is always polished and error-free.

Social Media Mastery: Crafting posts on LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, or Instagram? The extension helps you maintain a professional image across all your social platforms with perfectly corrected text.

Online Forms Made Easy: From job applications to contact forms, TextFixer helps you make the best impression when filling out important web forms and applications.

How It Works

Using TextFixer in your browser is incredibly simple. Just select any text on any webpage, right-click, and choose "Fix with TextFixer" from the context menu. Your text is instantly processed and the corrected version is automatically copied to your clipboard, ready to paste anywhere.

The extension works with the same powerful AI that drives our mobile apps, ensuring consistent, high-quality corrections across all your devices. Your API key works seamlessly across iOS, Android, and now Chrome – giving you a unified writing experience wherever you work.

Privacy and Security

Just like our mobile apps, the Chrome extension processes your text securely and doesn't store any of your content permanently. Your writing stays private, and corrections happen quickly through our encrypted API.

Cross-Platform Writing Ecosystem

With TextFixer now available on iOS and browsers (with Android coming soon), you can maintain perfect writing standards across every device and platform you use. Whether you're drafting on your phone, editing on your tablet, or writing on your computer, TextFixer ensures your communication is always professional and error-free.

The best part? Your existing API key works everywhere. If you're already using TextFixer on iOS, simply install the browser extension and enter the same access code. New users can get started at textfixer.co.uk and use their API key across all platforms.

The browser extension is available now through the Chrome Web Store and works with the same API key you use for our iOS Shortcut. Our Android app is also in development and will be launching soon, completing the TextFixer ecosystem across all your devices.

Ready to improve your web-based writing? Download the TextFixer browser extension today and experience seamless text correction across every website you visit.
