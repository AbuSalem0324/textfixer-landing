Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2025

At TextFixer, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our text correction service.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the service.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our service, including:

Personal Information

  • Email address (when you create an account)
  • Payment information (processed through our payment provider Stripe)

Usage Information

  • Statistics about your text correction usage (frequency, character count)
  • Information about your device and connection (IP address, browser type, operating system)

Text Content

The text you submit for correction is processed through our API and OpenAI's services. We maintain the following practices regarding your text:

  • Text is processed securely through encrypted connections
  • Text is not permanently stored after processing is complete
  • Text is not used to train AI models
  • Text is not shared with third parties except as required for the correction service

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our service
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Monitor usage patterns to optimize performance
  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and customer service requests
  • Protect against fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that help us deliver our service (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting providers)
  • OpenAI: The text you submit for correction is processed through OpenAI's API
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal information
  • Withdrawing consent where processing is based on consent
  • Requesting restriction of processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@textfixer.com.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@textfixer.com
