Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up the TextFixer shortcut?

After subscribing, you'll receive an access code. Download the shortcut from our Downloads page, then enter your access code when prompted during setup.

What's the difference between the free and pro plans?

The free plan offers 20 text fixes per month with a 500 character limit per fix. The pro plan includes unlimited fixes with no character limit and priority processing.

I lost my access code, how can I get it back?

Contact us with your registered email address and we'll resend your access code within 24 hours.

Does TextFixer work on Android?

Currently, TextFixer is only available for iOS devices. We're exploring Android support for future releases.
